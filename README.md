## Effects of Temperature Shocks and Dessication on Intertidal Crabs
Materials List
- Two tubs for hemigrapsus and green crab
- Thermometer for air/water temperature
- Scale
- Calipers
- 2 heat lamps
- 2 tray corrals
- Sandy substrate to fill corrals
- Resazurin salt, DI water, DMSO
- 4 cups for green crab resazurin & controls
- 2 6-cup plates for hemigrapsus resazurin & controls
- Two 96-well plates
- Tags to identify crabs
